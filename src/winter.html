Winter Running Series

Register

The Winter Running Series helps you train for either a Full Marathon or Half Marathon come springtime. This year we are excited to introduce a new four-part training series supported by race specific 18-week training plans, designed by Coach Lora Erickson of blonderunner.com. The Winter Running Series will help prepare you to run any distance in the Ogden Marathon, or train for other springtime running events! Register for the full series or individual events.

What Comes With The Training Series?

  • 4 Race Events – professional timing, aid stations, & finish line treats!
  • Participant Shirt – super soft unisex long sleeve tee
  • 4-part Finishers Medal – collect a piece at each event to make up the full medal

18-Week Training Plan – created by Coach Lora Erickson (available in early January). Separate registration required.

  • Full Marathon – Beginner, Intermediate, & Advanced options
  • Half Marathon – Beginner & Intermediate options
  • 12K & 5K

Invitation to join the private Facebook Group to access live Q&A sessions with Coach Lora Erickson, in-person clinics, meetup runs, and more!
