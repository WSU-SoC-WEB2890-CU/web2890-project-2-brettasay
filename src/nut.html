Calling All Local Musicians!

The Northern Utah Trailfest is on the lookout for amazing performers to take the stage on October 4th and 5th. If you’re passionate about music and love the great outdoors, this is your chance to shine! Whether you’re a solo artist, duo, or a band, we want to hear from you! Don’t miss out on this incredible opportunity to showcase your talent at one of Northern Utah’s most anticipated outdoor events! Link below to apply now or learn more. Applications close on April 1st.
